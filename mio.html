Welcome to MIO

Mining environmental monitoring system based on IoT to monitor temperature, humidity, air quality, light intensity, and alarm systems in real time.

What is MIO?

MIO (Mining IoT Overview) is an Internet of Things-based system designed to monitor mining environmental conditions in real time including temperature, humidity, gas levels, and light intensity to improve safety and efficiency.
